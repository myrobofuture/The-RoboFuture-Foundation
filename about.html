Our Mission

My mission is to empower passionate 5th to 8th grade students with the tools, support, and knowledge they need to succeed in robotics. Through live and self-paced courses, peer mentorship, and guidance on key skills like coding, building, and keeping an engineering notebook, I aim to make robotics more approachable and fun. How It Works

Students sign up for courses or mentorship sessions and follow a guided learning path that includes engineering notebook documentation, coding fundamentals, and hands-on building challenges. Our mentors provide feedback, guidance, and inspiration throughout the journey.
